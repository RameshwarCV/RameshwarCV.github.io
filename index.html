Rameshwar Mishra

I am a PhD student at IIIT Delhi, Advised by Prof. A. V. Subramanyam as part of the Visual Conception Group. My research interests lie in controllable content synthesis, scene-graph conditioned diffusion models, and generative visual understanding.

I have previously worked with Dr. Liang Zheng at the School of Computing in Australian National University, and collaborated with Dr. Bishshoy Das, Dr. Guan-Ming Su at Dolby Laboratories, and Dr. Srikrishna Karanam at Adobe Research.
